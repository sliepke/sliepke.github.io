Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 4 bell peppers
  • 6 carrots
  • Cooking oil
  • A lot of cumin
  • Some cayenne powder
  • 2 boxes (~4 cups each) vegetable broth
  • 1 little can (~6 oz) tomato paste
  • 2 cups dry brown lentils
  • 1 bag (~8-16 oz) of spinach

Steps

  • Sautee the onions, bell peppers, and carrots in cooking oil for a while. Optionally, begin without oil first to evaporate a bunch of the water without wasting oil fumes. The longer it sautees in oil, the more flavorful it will be (as long as it doesn't burn). When I have enough time, I go until the vegetables are a little brown.
  • Add the cumin and cayenne powder into the sautee for a little bit.
  • Add vegetable broth, tomato paste, and brown lentils. Simmer for a while, at least until the lentils are al dente. The longer it simmers the more flavorful.
  • Add the spinach and simmer until wilted.
