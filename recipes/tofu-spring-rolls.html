Tofu Spring Rolls

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Cornstarch
  • Savoy cabbage
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Choice of sauce (peanut sauce is what I use)
  • Choice of vegetables (baby cucumbers, carrots, etc)

Steps

Prepare tofu

  • Freeze tofu overnight, then defrost it
  • Gently squeeze tofu over sink to remove most of the water
  • Cut or rip tofu into pieces, then put it in a mixing bowl
  • Coat tofu in 1tbsp oil and 1tbsp soy sauce and stir
  • Coat tofu evenly with 1-2tbsp cornstarch and stir
  • Bake tofu on parchment paper at 400F for 25-30min

Make spring rolls

  • Prepare tofu, sauce if using (like peanut sauce), and any vegetables you're using
  • Make spring rolls (this is the hard part and takes practice)
    • Spring rolls have to be soaked in water for a few seconds before using
    • The tofu, sauce, and vegetables should be tightly wrapped in the cabbage, and the cabbage is wrapped in the spring roll wrapper
