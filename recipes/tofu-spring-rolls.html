Tofu Spring Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 block extra firm tofu
  • 1.5 tbsp cooking oil
  • Optional dry seasoning for tofu
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • Choice of sauce (peanut sauce is what I use)
  • Savoy cabbage (will use about 1/2 of a head, double recipe to use whole head)
  • Choice of vegetables to go in the spring rolls (cucumber, carrot, etc)
  • Spring roll wrappers

Steps

Prepare tofu

  • Preheat oven to 425F
  • Either:
    • Freeze, defrost, then lightly squeeze most of the water out of the tofu by hand (takes a few seconds to squeeze most of the water out once defrosted) (freezing leads to a slightly different texture and makes it absorb more flavor on the inside if you go an extra mile to marinade it)
    • Press most of the water out of the tofu (takes about 15 minutes)
  • Cut or rip tofu into pieces, then put it in a mixing bowl
  • Coat tofu in cooking oil and dry seasoning if using, and gently mix with hands
  • Coat tofu evenly in cornstarch, and gently mix with hands
  • Bake tofu on parchment paper for 30-40 minutes, flipping the pieces halfway

Make spring rolls

  • Prepare tofu, sauce if using (like peanut sauce), and any vegetables you're using
  • Make spring rolls (this is the hard part and takes practice)
    • Spring roll wrappers have to be soaked in water for a few seconds before using
    • The tofu, sauce, and vegetables are to be wrapped in cabbage leaves as compactly as possible, and the cabbage is to be wrapped in the spring roll wrapper
