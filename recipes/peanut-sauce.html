Peanut Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1+1/2 tbsp agave, or 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp chili garlic sauce
  • Juice of 1/4 lime
  • 3 grated or minced garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp grated or minced ginger

Steps

Mix all ingredients together in a bowl or container.

Add and mix 2-4 tbsp warm water until desired consistency.
