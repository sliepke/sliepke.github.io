Peanut Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1+1/2 tbsp agave, or 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2tsp chili garlic sauce
  • 1/4 lime juice
  • 3 grated or minced garlic cloves
  • 1tb grated or minced ginger

Steps

Put all ingredients together in a bowl or container, then mix and add 2-4tbsp warm water until desired consistency
